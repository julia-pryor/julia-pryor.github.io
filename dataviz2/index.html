The Banning of Books in U.S. Schools

Over 1,649 books were banned in schools across the U.S. from July 1, 2021 to June 30, 2022. While most books were only banned once or twice, the ones included in this visualization were banned over a dozen or more times. This is especially concerning since the top 5 most banned books include content about sexuality, gender expression, and/or racial inequality. This suggests that biases could be a motive behind book bans.
